Asian Foodie in Gdansk, Poland

26-11-2017
Food, Poland, Gdansk

I have been staying here in Gdansk for more than 3months already. There are some Asian food selling in local supermarkets here. However, not all of them are good. This post will be showing some of them that I have tried and commenting them.

Noodles

Makaron pszenny, świeży-Udon

From: Korea

Comment:

After cooking in soup (3min by instruction), the taste of Udon and soup become sour. It happens usually when it is “BAD” udon. Besides, it is difficult to separate the Udon during cooking without breaking it.

If you really miss Asian Udon, go for it, else, you might find better choices.

(Haven’t tried stir-fry way yet)
